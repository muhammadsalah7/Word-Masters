Rules

Each day a new 5 letter word is generated and it's called "word of the day"

You have 6 chances to geuss the word of the day

If the letter is green this means you got the right letter in the right order

If the letter is yellow this means you got the right letter but in the wrong order

If the letter is red this means that the letter is not in this word

You can only enter valid 5 letter words
